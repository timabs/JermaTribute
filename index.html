Who is Jerma?

His journey began in 2011, on YouTube, as a Team Fortress 2 commentator. Jerma's channel grew quickly as he capitalized on the game's popularity & demand for content. However, Jerma never pigeonholed himself into just the one game. As he got more comfortable being his true self in his videos, his personality began to shine & attract a larger audience. Support for TF2 slowed down & the game received fewer updates causing Jerma to lose interest. Around 2016, Jerma switched to livestreaming on Twitch as YouTube had become quite unfriendly to gaming content creators of his ilk. However, no matter what platform he was on, Jerma followed his passions & exercised his creativity in bold ways. Jerma's streams in particular pushed, & continue to push, the boundaries of livestreaming. The content creation space widely recognizes him as an innovator, yet Jerma remains down-to-earth & uniquely able to connect with his audience. His longevity is proof enough of his dedication to making high-quality content and fostering one of the most vibrant communities on the internet.
